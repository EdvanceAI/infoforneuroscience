About Us

We are a youth-led organization that explores the effects of music and non-pharmaceutical treatments on mental health. Through research, educational posts, trivia, and our podcast “Info For Neuroscience: Harmony & Knowledge,” we aim to make neuroscience accessible and engaging.

Our mission at Info For Neuroscience (IFN) is to raise awareness about the impact of neuroscience on mental well-being, using research, education, and therapeutic tools to create positive change in communities
